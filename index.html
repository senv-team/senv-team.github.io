[side project]

We are a group of people who wanted to start a new side project. Instead of sticking ourselves onto the road to help raise awareness of climate change, we want to do our part with analysing data and hopefully find something useful!

[collaboration]

We meet once a week for an hour to work together and update each other on what progress we made. If you feel like this is something you'd be interested in, say hi and we can see whether you can get involved (core team, short-term collaborator, ...).
